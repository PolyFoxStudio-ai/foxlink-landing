Features

Built for teams who sell.
Not teams who manage software.

Every feature in FoxLink earns its place. Nothing is there to look good on a comparison chart — it's there because it saves you time.

📋
Custom pipelines
Build up to four pipelines — name each one and define your own kanban columns. Run sales, renewals, and delivery side by side. Deal health indicators flag anything going quiet or slipping past close date.
👥
Contacts & companies
Unlimited contacts linked to companies. Full activity history, AI summaries, lead scores, and LinkedIn URLs. Import your entire contact list from CSV in minutes.
📅
Activity logging
Log calls, emails, meetings, and notes in seconds. Paste rough call notes and let AI clean them into a structured summary with action items — right inside the modal.
✅
Tasks
Create tasks linked to contacts, deals, or pipeline cards. Assign to team members. Overdue tasks surface at the top every morning so nothing gets missed. AI ranks your day around them.
🗓️
Calendar
A full calendar with day, week, month, and agenda views. Create events, invite contacts and team members, and see all your tasks alongside your meetings in one place.
🗂️
Card task boards & custom fields
Every pipeline card gets its own internal task kanban for the work behind the deal, plus custom fields to store exactly the information you need. AI gives each pipeline a health update based on a description you set.
📬
Web intake forms
Build a lead capture form and embed it on your website. Submissions land directly in your CRM as new contacts — no Zapier, no manual import, no delay.
👤
Team workspace
Invite your team, assign account managers, and see shared pipeline and activity in one place. Audit log tracks every change so nothing happens without a trace.
📊
Reports & exports
Pipeline reports, contact lists, company summaries, and activity logs — all exportable as PDF or CSV in seconds. Always up to date, no manual work required.

FAQ

Questions answered.

How does the free trial work?
Sign up with your email — no card needed. You get 14 days of full access to every feature including all AI tools. After the trial, upgrade to Pro or your data stays safe and you can come back any time.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running in under an hour. Import your contacts from CSV, configure your pipeline stages, and you're selling the same day. No consultant required, no lengthy onboarding.
What is the daily agenda email?
FoxLink's AI writes you a personalised briefing every morning — your top-priority contacts ranked by urgency, deals closing soon, overdue tasks, and what your team logged yesterday. Arrives before 7am. Toggle it off any time in Settings.
How do web intake forms work?
Build a form in FoxLink and embed it on your website. When someone fills it in, they're automatically added as a contact in your workspace — no Zapier, no manual import, no delay.
Is my data safe and GDPR compliant?
Yes. FoxLink runs on EU-hosted infrastructure in the London region with row-level security — your workspace data is completely isolated. We're registered with the ICO and comply fully with UK GDPR. We never sell or share your data.
We already have a CRM.
Most teams do. The question is whether your team actually uses it. FoxLink is built to be the CRM your reps open every morning — not the one they update when they have to. Try it for 14 days and see.
Can it integrate with our other tools?
CSV import and export mean your data is never locked in. Calendar sync and Zapier integration are on the roadmap. We build integrations based on what our customers actually use — get in touch to influence what comes next.
What happens when my trial ends?
You can upgrade to Pro at any time from Settings → Billing. If you don't upgrade, you'll move to read-only access — your data is safe and you can come back whenever you're ready.
