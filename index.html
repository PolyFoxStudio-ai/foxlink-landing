FAQ

Questions? Answered.

How does the free trial work?
Sign up with your email — no card needed. You get 14 days of full access to every feature including all AI tools. After the trial, upgrade to Pro or your data stays safe and you can come back any time.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running in under an hour. Create your workspace, import your contacts via CSV, and you're selling the same day. No consultant required.
What is the daily agenda email?
FoxLink's AI writes you a personalised briefing every morning — your top-priority contacts ranked by urgency, deals closing soon, overdue tasks, and what your team logged yesterday. Arrives before 7am.
How do web intake forms work?
Create a form in FoxLink and embed it on your website. When someone fills it in, they're automatically added as a contact in your workspace — no Zapier, no manual import.
Is my data safe and GDPR compliant?
Yes. FoxLink runs on EU-hosted infrastructure (London region) with row-level security — your workspace data is completely isolated. We comply with UK GDPR and do not sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can it integrate with our other tools?
CSV import and export mean your data is never locked in. Zapier integration and calendar sync are on the roadmap. We build integrations based on what our customers actually use — get in touch to influence the roadmap.
We already have a CRM.
Most teams do. The question is whether your team actually uses it. FoxLink is built to be the CRM your reps open every morning — not the one they update when they have to. Try it for 14 days and see.
What happens when my trial ends?
You can upgrade to Pro at any time from your account settings. If you don't upgrade, you'll move to read-only access — your data is safe and you can come back whenever you're ready.
